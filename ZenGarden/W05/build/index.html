Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

Even Though Zen Garden has been specially created for all type of designers, our Group#2 Zen Design will be focused on fresh, young designers giving a glance of spring season through the whole year.

Our main theme is “Nature” and all the beauty that you can find on earth. You’ll be able to find hints of nature, from the color palette to the images, and logo that we have chosen. We believe “The Nature” is one of the biggest inspirations any designer could have to create, and innovate new designs.

We were inspired by Japan where the Zen Garden is popular. That is why we choose a Sakura for our main theme.
